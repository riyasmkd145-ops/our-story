A Letter

June 2026

Dear Ammu,

I have been sitting with this letter for a long time, not knowing where to begin. But I know I owe you words — honest, heartfelt words — before we truly walk our separate paths.

Eight years. Eight years of memories, of laughter, of quiet moments, of growing up together. You were not just a part of my life — you were a chapter I will carry with me forever. And for all of that, I am deeply, truly grateful.

Thank you for the warmth you brought into my days. Thank you for the patience you showed me when I didn't deserve it. Thank you for believing in me, for standing by me, and for loving me the way you did. You gave me so much more than I ever acknowledged in the moment, and I am sorry it took losing you for me to see that clearly.

I am sorry. I am sorry for the times I fell short. For the moments I was not present enough, not careful enough with your feelings, not the person you needed me to be. You deserved better, and I carry that truth with me — not with guilt, but with the hope that it makes me grow.

Wherever life takes you from here, I hope it brings you every happiness you have ever dreamed of. I hope the days ahead are kinder to you than I sometimes was, and I mean that with all my heart — not just with words.

This is my final message to you, Ammu.

I will not call. I will not try to reach you or find ways to stay connected. Not because I don't care — but because I care too much to make your new beginning harder than it needs to be. You deserve peace. You deserve to step into your future without the weight of my presence pulling at the edges.

Thank you for everything, Ammu. For the love, the lessons, all the memories that still makes me smile. I will carry them all with me, always.

Take care of yourself. Be happy. You deserve it more than you know.

With love, always, ♡
🌸

Wishing you
all the happiness
in the world.

Every day, in every way.

2018 — 2026
